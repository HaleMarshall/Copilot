Moonfare AI Copilot Use Cases
Framing
The goal is not to build dozens of isolated AI features.
The goal is to build a private markets operating system that helps an investor:
construct a portfolio
compare it to meaningful benchmarks
understand what is happening inside it
forecast what is likely to happen next
identify the best next specific action
evaluate named opportunities
compare herself/himself with peers
learn from past decisions
use Moonfare’s internal market, demand, and investor-intelligence data
The key reason Moonfare can do this in a differentiated way is that the underlying data is dense and proprietary:
Core proprietary Moonfare data
investor profile, investment targets, risk appetite, profession, geography, family/life-stage, age, wealth bucket
investor holdings, commitments, ticket sizes, transaction history, vintage activity
fund performance, NAV, calls, distributions, deployment and exit histories
private fund portfolio company data and direct investment look-through
PPMs, LPAs, DD materials, IC materials, recommendation logic
platform demand, oversubscription, fill rates, allocation outcomes
internal secondary market liquidity and pricing histories
sales and advisory call transcripts, investor questions, sentiment, intent signals
Moonfare model portfolios and advisory frameworks
historical Moonfare opportunities the investor did or did not invest in
Most important external data
macro indicators: GDP, inflation, rates, energy, credit spreads, FX
market, news, and thematic developments
Moonfare model portfolios
Moonfare macro view
